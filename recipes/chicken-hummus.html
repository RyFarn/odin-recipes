Chicken Hummus Bowl

Description

Ever fed up of the usual boring sandwiches for lunch? Well give this incredible chicken hummus bowl a try, and inject some variety into your food. This incredibly simple recipe is quick, easy to make, and packed full of nutrition. Those stale sandwiches will be a distant memory after this!

Ingredients

  • 200g hummus
  • 1 lemon
  • 200g cooked mixed grains
  • 150g baby spinach
  • 1 avocado
  • 1 cooked chicken breast
  • 100g pomegranate seeds
  • 1/2 red onion
  • 2 tbsp toasted almonds

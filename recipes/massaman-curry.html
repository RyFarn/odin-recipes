Massaman Curry

A delicious beef massman curry in a bowl

Description

Try this delicious beef massaman curry recipe today. Inspired from the amazing cuisine of southeast asia, this dish is undoubtedly one of the best curries in the world. Packed full of nutritious vegetables, tender beef and a flavourful curry sauce. Try it, you'll be glad you did!

Ingredients

  • 85 unsalted peanuts
  • 400ml can coconut milk
  • 4 tbsp massaman curry paste
  • 600g stewing steak
  • 450g potatoes
  • 1 onion
  • 4 kaffir lime
  • 1 cinnamon stick
  • 1 tbsp tamarind paste
  • 1 tbsp brown sugar
  • 1 tbsp fish sauce
  • 1 red chilli
  • jasmine rice, to serve

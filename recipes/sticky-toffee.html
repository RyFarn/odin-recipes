Sticky Toffee Pudding

Description

There's always room for dessert, especially if it's this one! The only downside to making this is that once you've tasted it, every other dessert will pale in comparison. So, forget about trying to be good, because this time, it's time to be bad. Enjoy!

Ingredients

  • 225g medjool dates
  • 175ml boiling water
  • tbsp vanilla extract
  • 175g self-raising flower
  • 1tsp bicarbonate of soda
  • 2 eggs
  • 135g butter
  • 140g demerera sugar
  • 3tbsp black treacle
  • 100ml milk
  • 175g muscovado sugar
  • 225ml double cream
